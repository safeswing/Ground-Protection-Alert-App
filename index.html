Ground Protection Alert (GPA) – Game Overview

Ground Protection Alert (GPA) is a free, fan-made survival horror game inspired by the SCP Foundation universe. Built completely from scratch in Unity, GPA brings a fresh take on the classic SCP: Containment Breach experience, with updated visuals, improved mechanics, and a new storyline.

You take on the role of a Class-E test subject, assigned to assist in dangerous experiments in the Heavy Containment Zone, testing on SCP-173. But containment never lasts forever. The deeper you go, the more you’ll discover about the Foundation’s hidden projects, experimental procedures, and the truth behind your own role in the facility.

Can you survive long enough to uncover the full lore?

Features:

  • SCP Atmosphere Remade: Faithfully recreated environments using textures, sounds, and assets inspired by SCP: Containment Breach.
  • Survival Mechanics: Classic blink and stamina systems.
  • Exploration & Horror: Navigate locked-down corridors, security checkpoints, and experimental chambers.
  • Lore Discovery: Collect documents, observe experiments, and uncover hidden mysteries.
  • Unity Rebuild: Fully remade engine for smoother performance, modern graphics, and expandable development.

Development & Future Plans:

GPA is currently in early development, starting with SCP-173 testing scenarios. Future updates will expand the roster of SCPs, environments, and story content, eventually evolving into a full containment-breach survival experience.

  • Android versions are not available yet (planned for the future).
  • Player feedback will help shape the game as it develops.
